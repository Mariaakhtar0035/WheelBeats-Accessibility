Restrooms

  • All washrooms are wheelchair accessible
  • Accessible family servicees washrooms are located on all levels

Accessible Parking

  • Budweiser Gardens offers Accessible Parking in the City of London parking lot off of King Street (0.1 km from Gate 1 and Gate 2)
  • There are 12 accessibility spots available for every event (cannot be reserved, first come first serve parking)
  • The parking is close to Gate 2 and Gate 1 for entry

Entrances and Elevators

  • All gates are accessible
  • There are 3 public elevators inside the arena at gate 1 and 4

Additional Facilities

  • All drinking fountains are accessible
  • Concession stands are at an accesible height
  • All public telephones are wheelchair accessible
