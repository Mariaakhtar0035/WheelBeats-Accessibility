Contact Information

  • Book tickets via Ticketmaster
  • Address: 1663 Queen St E, Toronto, ON M4L 1G5
  • Website: History Toronto

Accessible Seating

  • The Accessible Seating Area is located on the Mezzanine level, every other area is standing only
  • Tickets for the Accessible Seating Area (ASA) are available for purchase on Ticketmaster
  • Virtual Venue: View

Restrooms

  • Restrooms on the ground floor and the Mezzanine are equipped with barrier-free stalls
  • Two barrier-free washrooms on the ground floor are available

Elevators and Ramps

  • Ramps and elevators are availible
  • The ramp will provide access to the Coat Check and the Lower General Admission (GA) area
  • The elevator, located in the south-east corner of the venue, will provide access to the Mezzanine level where the Accessible Seating Area (ASA) is located
  • Wheelchairs are able to access the General Admission Floor areas of the ground level and the Mezzanine

Accessible Parking

  • 1167 Eastern Avenue, behind the venue (should be purchased with your ticket to reserve a parking spot)

Accessible Entrance

  • All main entrances to the venue are at street level and free of steps
