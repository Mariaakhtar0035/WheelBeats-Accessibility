Rogers Centre

Contact

  • Visit ticketmaster for tickets
  • Call 416-341-1000 for accessible parking and other inquiries
  • Call 647-559-0060 for assistance during the concert
  • Address: 1 Blue Jays Way, Toronto, ON M5V 1J1
  • Website: Rogers Centre

Accessible Seating

  • All accesible seating indicated
  • Sections 108-115, 121-127, 134-139, 241, 207 also suites available for rent
  • Staff available to take you to and from your seat - call 1 day in advance to book
  • Up to 3 guests allowed with the person with disability
  • Accessible Seating Map
  • Virtual Venue: View

Restrooms

  • All restroom are accessible
  • Accessible multipurpose washrooms are located in sections 134, 238 and 539
  • Universal washroom in section 117
  • Universal washroom includes a barrier-free toilet and sink, turning space to accommodate a wheeled mobility device, entrance with a power door, grab bars, emergency call system, and an adult size change table.

Accessible Entrance

  • 7 is the designated accessible entrance on the southside close to Bremner Boulevard allows for more time when checking in
  • All gates except 3 are accessible, 7 is just the easiest access
  • Escorts are available at the main gate (7)
  • Elevators located at: gates 3, 7, 9, 13

Additional Information

  • All payphones, ATMs are accessible
  • guest services are located on the 100-Level near Sections 120 and 136, on the 200-Level near Sections 212 and 236 and on the 500-Level near Sections 508 and 532
  • Outlets available at fan services for charging wheelchair
