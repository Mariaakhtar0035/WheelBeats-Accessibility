Accessible Seating

  • Accessible seating [all seating indicated by W]: Accessible seating is available on the concourse level (top of level 100) and can be accessed via elevator and/or escalator
  • Level 100 and level 200 seating sections are not wheelchair accessible

Restrooms

  • Accessible restrooms available

Accessible Parking

  • Accessible parking [2m high parking garage]: Convention Center Parking (on Summers Lane), 0.2 km from the venue

Accessible Entrance

  • Main entrance is accessible
  • Wheelchair escort: Guest Relations desk at Aisle 37 to speak with an Event Services Supervisor
