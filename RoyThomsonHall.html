Roy Thomson Hall

Contact

  • Visit ticketmaster for tickets
  • Call (416) 872-4255 for inquiries
  • Address: 60 Simcoe St, Toronto, ON M5J 2H5
  • Website: Roy Thomson Hall

Accessible Seating

  • All levels of Roy Thomson Hall are wheelchair-accessible
  • Accessible seating locations may be purchased by contacting the box office or on Ticketmaster

Restrooms

  • Accessible restrooms are available via the central elevator from all levels

Accessible Parking

  • 2m high parking garage
  • There is accessible parking available at the rear of Roy Thomson Hall via Wellington Street
  • 23 spots available in the surface parking lot
  • 6 parking spots in the underground parking lot

Accessible Entrance

  • Patrons with accessibility needs should arrive via the main entrance on Simcoe Street
