Scotiabank Arena

Contact

  • Address: 40 Bay St., Toronto, ON M5J 2X2
  • Phone: (416) 815-5500
  • Wesbite: Scotiabank Arena

Accessible Seating

  • Small balcony sections without fixed seats (100, 300, 600 levels)
  • Regular seats are stair access exclusively
  • Some guests recommend the lower level accessible seating if possible (100’s) as it is less restricted (600’s can be obstructed)
  • It is advised to not buy a general admission standing ticket because visibility will be limited and you may be asked to move towards the back of the area for evacutation safety
  • Seating Maps: 100 Level 300 Level
  • Virtual Venue: View

Restrooms

  • All washrooms are wheelchair accessible

Ticket Information

  • Up to 4 seats may be purchased to accommodate the accessible needs fan, a support person and/or guests
  • Call (855) 682-6736 for inquiries to book accessible seating (or Ticketmaster "W” class seats)

Accessible Parking

  • 4 onsite accessible parking spaces for fans with disabilities
  • Reserve up to 4 weeks in advance, reservations are required and early is good
  • Call (416) 815-5982 during business hours
  • Parking garage will open one hour prior to the event
  • The height of the parking garage is 6’6” (approximately 2 meters)
  • Underground parking at Scotiabank Arena is $50 for an event night (price subject to change). Scotiabank Arena is a cashless venue, only credit or debit payments will be accepted.

Accessible Entrance

  • Accessible entrance: gate 1 galleria
  • Wheelchair escort availible, notify the front desk after arrival if needed
  • Fans being picked up or dropped off by Wheel-Trans or private vehicles should use the west side Gate 5/6 area, in the Bremner Boulevard traffic loop.
