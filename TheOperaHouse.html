The Opera House Toronto

Contact Information

  • Call (416) 466-0313 for inquiries
  • Book tickets via Ticketmaster
  • Address: 735 Queen St E, Toronto, ON M4M 1H1
  • Website: The Opera House

Accessible Seating

  • Every show has 12 reserved seats for every show (call ahead get accessible seating reserved)

Restrooms

  • The private washroom measures 35 inches
  • There are no automatic features and the door must be opened and closed using a circular door handle
  • The total stall measures 65 x 72 inches, with two grab bars, one horizontal and one vertical, each measuring 32 inches
  • This washroom is reserved only for those with accessibility requirements

Accessible Parking

  • No onsite parking, but accessible parking nearby
  • The nearest parking lot is located at 111 Broadview Avenue, an estimated 2-minute walk to the venue

Accessible Entrance

  • Main entrance is accessible, and portable ramps are available upon request
  • Two sets of double-doors (58 inches) give way to a ramp leading into the venue
  • On concert nights, security guards are holding the doors open and are available to assist people up the ramp should they find the gradient steep
  • Once up the ramp, another set of double-doors (58 inches) leads you into the back level of the venue, where the bar (43 inches high), accessible seating, and a private washroom are located.

Additional Information

  • The venues interior is covered in hard, flat stone that is easily traversed
