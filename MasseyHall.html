Massey Hall

Contact Information

  • Call (416) 872-4255 for inquiries
  • Book tickets via Ticketmaster
  • If you'd like to do advance booking, email frontofhouse@mh-rth.com
  • Address: 178 Victoria St, Toronto, ON M5B 1T7
  • Website: Massey Hall

Accessible Seating

  • All levels are wheelchair accessible with elevators are available for those with accessible needs
  • Level 100 has the most wheelchair accessible seating

Restrooms

  • Main Floor (Level 100) restrooms are located on the lower level in the Muskoka Spirits Basement Bar, accessible by the front lobby lift (ask usher for assistance)
  • Balcony (Level 200) and Gallery (Level 300) are available on the seating level, in the Grolsch Bars on Level 2 & 3.

Accessible Parking

  • No on-site parking but accessible parking lots for nearby buildings

Accessible Entrance

  • Elevators access and Seating to Level 200 and 300 is via the 178 Victoria Street Entrance
  • Seats on Level 100 should enter through Front Entrance
