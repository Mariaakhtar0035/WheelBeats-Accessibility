BMO Field

Contact Information

  • Call (855) 985-4625 for inquiries
  • Book tickets via Ticketmaster
  • Address: 170 Princes' Blvd, Toronto, ON M6K 3C3
  • Website: BMO Field

Accessible Seating

  • Wheelchair sections are designated with an “A” following the section number (eg. 124A, 321A, and 325A - which must be entered from Gate 5)
  • Accessible sections have no outter barriers and removable seating that can accommodate wheelchairs if needed
  • Up to four (4) seats may be purchased at one time in an accessible section
  • Virtual Venue: View

Restrooms

  • Every restroom in the stadium is accessible

Accessible Parking

  • Exhibition Place Lot 2, located just south of Gate 3
  • People being picked up or dropped off by private vehicle or Wheel-Trans may do so at Gate 1 (near the Ticket Office) or Gate 3 (just off Princes’ Blvd)

Accessible Entrance

  • Gate 5 is where those with accessibility seats should go (closest to your seats)
