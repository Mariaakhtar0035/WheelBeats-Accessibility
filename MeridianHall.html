Accessible Seating

  • The orchestra and mezzanine levels are dedicated to accessible seating locations
  • Other seating suitable for patrons with limited mobility

Restrooms

  • Fully accessible washrooms are located in the lower lobby and on the main floor of Meridian Hall

Accessible Parking

  • No onsite parking (accessible or otherwise)
  • Closest parking lots are driving distance away (>1 km)

Accessible Entrance

  • All entrances are accessible
