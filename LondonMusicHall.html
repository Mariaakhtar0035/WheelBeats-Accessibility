Accessible Seating

  • Only the main floor is accessible
  • Rum Runners is not accessible
  • The London Music Hall Mezzanine & Balcony section are not wheelchair accessible (no passenger elevator available)
  • Balcony Rows are reservable and wheelchair accessible
  • General floor is standing only

Restrooms

  • Restrooms are large, but it is unclear whether they are fully wheelchair accessible

Accessible Parking

  • There is a municipal lot right outside the venue doors on Queens Ave (0.5 km)

Accessible Entrance

  • Main entrance is accessible
